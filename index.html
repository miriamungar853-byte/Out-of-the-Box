About Out of the Box

Out of the Box is a tablescape rental company that brings elevated, curated tabletop designs to home celebrations. We make it easy to host beautifully without the stress of sourcing linens, chargers, dinnerware, glassware and décor piece by piece. Choose from thoughtfully styled collections that match your vibe, then let us help you create a cohesive, photo-ready table for birthdays, Sheva Brochos, brunches, upsherin and anniversaries. Whether you want subtle and modern or bold and festive, our rentals turn everyday get-togethers into memorable moments—out of the box and onto the table.
